About Michael

A jack of few trades...
Michael, coming from a small family started playing music at a young age. Michael later went on to study in the Cork Academy of Music. Here he studied Music Theory and perfected his playing. Michael is now involved in many aspects of the music industry. HE now regularly produces music for Licensing, in Film, Advertising, Wedding Videos, etc... He also preforms his music under Factotem and his own name, and much much more.
